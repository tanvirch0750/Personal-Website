About me

Hi, my name is Tanvir Chowdhury! I'm a front end developer. My job is to build your website so that it is functional and user-friendly but at the same time attractive.

I have a strong passion for the creativity and discipline that goes into front-end development. Keeping in mind scalability and clean code that is maintainable and readable. I’ve worked with companies of all shapes and sizes, so contact me if you’d like to work together on your next project.
